My Hobby

Gym

I was introduced to gym by my friend who was into body building. At first, I was totally against it because it would make me too heavy for soccer but I still went ahead and tried doing 50 push-ups a day. That's when I started seeing some positive changes in my torso and that was when I got hooked for good. Now is feels like home simply because even though it brings with it a lot of pain , it also has helped with my confidence, discipline and mental health so I find comfort in being there, working out.

My top 3 exercises are:

  1. Incline bench press
  2. Bicep curls
  3. Leg extensions

My least favourite exercises include:

  • Jumping lunges
  • Deadlifts
  • Shoulder press
